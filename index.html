What is the Book of Mormon?

The Book of Mormon is another testament of Jesus Christ, alongside the Bible. It is a sacred volume of scripture that records God's dealings with ancient inhabitants of the Americas and bears powerful testimony of the Savior.

Why is it Important?

This book answers life’s greatest questions, brings individuals closer to God, and provides a clear witness of Jesus Christ. It invites all people to follow Him, learn His teachings, and find peace through His gospel.

Jesus Christ: The Central Figure

Above all, the Book of Mormon testifies of Jesus Christ. He is mentioned on nearly every page. His life, ministry, Atonement, and resurrection are at the heart of its message.

“And we talk of Christ, we rejoice in Christ, we preach of Christ, we prophesy of Christ...”
— 2 Nephi 25:26
